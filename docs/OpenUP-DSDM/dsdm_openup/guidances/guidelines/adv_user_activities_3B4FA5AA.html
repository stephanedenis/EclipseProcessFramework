Typical Phase by Phase Activities

Inception Phase

  • Participate in workshops to create the project Vision if requested by the Visionary.
  • Agree to participate at the level requested throughout the project.

Elaboration Phase

  • Participate in Workshops to generate requirements if requested by the Visionary.
  • Provide business information if requested by the Ambassador User.
  • Participate in prototype reviews and workshops if requested by the Ambassador User.
  • Participate in testing if requested by the Ambassador User.
  • Provide feedback to the Ambassador User about the system under development.

Construction Phase

  • Participate in testing and reviews if requested by the Ambassador User.

Transition Phase

  • Participate in training and provide feedback on the training, the system and the user documentation.
  • Use the system and provide feedback through the specified channels.
  • Participate in post-live reviews if requested by the Visionary.
